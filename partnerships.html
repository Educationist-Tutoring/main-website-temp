Partnerships

We partner with other non-profit organizations to help bring accessible education to as many people as possible

Sevalaya

Sevalaya has helped over 1 million people in India during the pandemic by providing food, ventilators, and other relief packages. We partnered with them to help provide Education to children in rural villages in India.

Sevalaya

Nanritam

Nanritam is a non-profit social welfare organization guided by the ideals of service to mankind of Swami Vivekananda. They perform service in areas of India with high poverty rates, where they provide volunteer relief work, including skill-training and education. We have partnered with Nanritam to help provide education to students in poor areas in India.

Nanritam

Filix School

The Filix School of Education is an educational project that was set up by Nanritam, one of our other partners. The aim of this project is to provide an English-medium educational experience for students in a remote, rural area in India. They believe that not only developing the literacy of children is important in India, but being able to gain an education with western teaching methods allows for the students of the Filix School to have opportunities that they otherwise wouldn't have. We have teamed up with the Filix school to help provide an English-medium education to children in rural India to help increase their literacy and to open up new opportunities to them.
